What the show is about

Every week the show breaks down how search actually works and how to stay visible as Google and AI answer engines reshape discovery. Episodes cover technical SEO, content and keyword strategy, link building, local and ecommerce SEO, analytics, and the fast-moving world of AI search and LLM visibility.

The format is practical and operator-first: real tactics, named guests, and a bias toward what moves rankings and revenue rather than what trends on social.

Matthew Bertram, an AI keynote speaker, creator of DIG® (Digital Information Governance), and CEO of EWR Digital. He is President of ModalPoint, Chief Marketing Officer of the Oil and Gas Global Network (OGGN), and the author of eight books including LLM Visibility.
