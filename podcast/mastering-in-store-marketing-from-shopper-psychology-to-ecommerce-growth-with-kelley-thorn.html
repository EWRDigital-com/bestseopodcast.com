Questions this episode answers

How did Kelley Thornton grow his DTC brand Tiege using YouTube?

Thornton partnered with YouTube content creator Aaron Marino in 2016 and sponsored long-form content aimed at men across lifestyle topics. This top-of-funnel investment built branded traffic over time, and the company then captured that demand with paid search and paid social at the mid and lower funnel.

Why does Kelley Thornton discourage frequent discounting for DTC brands?

Thornton argues that running regular sales trains consumers to wait for discounts rather than buying at full price. He prefers adding items or value to a promotion instead of cutting the price, and only discounts for trial because the subscription economics of his business make it worthwhile.

What is the future 12-month earning model Tiege uses for customer acquisition?

Tiege estimates the expected revenue from each new customer over the next 12 months based on what product the customer bought, the price paid, and the channel they came from. The company then sets its acquisition spend against that prediction and tracks customer cohorts at 30, 45, 60, and 90 days to verify the model.

What are the 10-foot, 5-foot, and 1-foot principles in in-store marketing?

These are psychological distance principles used in retail. At 10 feet, colors and visuals grab consumer attention. At 5 feet, messaging draws shoppers close enough to touch the product. At 1 foot, clear communication of functional benefits closes the purchase decision while the product is in the shopper's hand.

Terms defined in this episode
Future 12-month earning
A predictive metric Tiege uses to estimate revenue a new customer will generate over 12 months, based on purchase history and acquisition channel, used to set daily spending limits.
Shopper marketing
The practice of applying psychological principles to influence consumer purchase decisions within a retail environment, including packaging, placement, signage, and display.
Top of funnel
The awareness stage of the marketing funnel; Thornton describes YouTube long-form content as a top-of-funnel play that builds brand recognition before lower-funnel paid acquisition channels convert prospects.
Contribution dollars
A profit metric Thornton tracks down to the channel and creative level to understand how much each dollar of spending actually contributes to the business.
