Questions this episode answers

How useful are meta tags for SEO today?

Meta tags are less important than they used to be, with content now carrying more weight. However, the description tag still matters because Google often displays it as the snippet in search results, making it a direct opportunity to pitch to searchers before they even visit the site.

What makes a backlink valuable or harmful to a website?

Relevance is the key factor. A backlink from a page whose content matches your site's topic adds credibility, especially when the anchor text includes relevant keywords. A link from an unrelated site, such as a pornography site pointing to a web design page, can result in a Google penalty.

Why do calls to action matter so much in SEO strategy?

Driving traffic to a site means nothing if visitors cannot find a way to act. The hosts explain that if a buy button or contact option is not immediately obvious, visitors will leave within about 10 seconds and purchase from a competitor instead, wasting all the SEO effort.

How does internal site structure affect Google PageRank?

According to the hosts, improper internal linking can decrease a page's Google PageRank. There is a maximum PageRank a site can reach, and poor link structure pulls scores below that ceiling. Good structure also helps visitors navigate easily, reducing bounce rates.

Terms defined in this episode
Backlink
All websites other than your own that link to a particular page. Relevance of the linking page and the anchor text used both affect how much SEO value the backlink provides.
Meta Description Tag
An HTML meta tag whose content Google often uses as the descriptive snippet shown beneath a search result, giving site owners a chance to pitch directly to searchers.
Google PageRank
A ranking signal named after Google co-founder Larry Page. Each page has a maximum possible PageRank, and improper internal linking can reduce a page's actual rank below that maximum.
Call to Action
An element on a webpage, such as a buy button or phone number, that prompts visitors to take a specific next step. Without clear calls to action, traffic does not convert into customers.
