How to Choose the Right SEO Company: 32 Questions to Ask by Adam Heitzman #375

To vet an SEO agency, ask about ranking guarantees, link building methods, reporting frequency, tools used, and measurable results like leads and closed business, not just traffic metrics.

Key takeaways
  • No agency can guarantee a specific ranking position because no one controls Google's algorithm.
  • Good link building uses relevant guest posts and legitimate directory submissions, not mass submissions or Fiverr services.
  • Clients who spend at least three hours a month engaging with their agency see significantly better campaign results.
  • Agencies should review reports live with clients monthly, not just email blind data dumps.
  • Upon launching a comprehensive campaign, clients typically see a 30 to 40 percent increase in leads within the first 30 days.
If you're going to Fiverr for link building, you may be paying $5 for the links. You may be paying $5,000 to fix them.

About this episode

Did you vet your SEO agency? Do you know how your agency handles guarantees, link building, site audits, and most importantly, results? We'll talk 3 Tips for finding a phenomenal SEO agency and a list of more than 30 questions to ask to make sure your SEO agency is on the level.

Questions this episode answers

Can an SEO agency guarantee a top ranking on Google?

No agency can guarantee a specific ranking position because no one has access to or control over Google's algorithm. However, eWebResults backs their process with a financial guarantee of up to $10,000, based on confidence that consistent good strategies produce ranking improvements over time.

What link building methods should a legitimate SEO agency use?

A legitimate agency should use relevant guest posting, citations, and directory submissions targeted to the most relevant and high-ranking directories. Mass submissions to thousands of directories or cheap link services like Fiverr are bad strategies that can require expensive disavow work to fix.

How often should an SEO agency send reports and communicate results?

Clients should receive a monthly call to review the previous month's data, not just a blind emailed report. eWebResults schedules a fixed monthly results call of about 45 minutes to an hour, often using Google Hangouts for screen sharing, with some clients on weekly calls.

In their words

We can't guarantee what Google will put your ranking at, but we can guarantee our process will get you great ranking. And we back that with the financial guarantee.
