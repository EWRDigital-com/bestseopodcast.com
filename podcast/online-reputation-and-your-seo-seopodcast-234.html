Questions this episode answers

How does Google filter restaurant search results on Android?

According to this episode, Google now filters Android restaurant search results based on price, cuisine type, number of reviews, and hours of operation. If a restaurant is closed, it will not appear in results, making accurate and updated listings critical for local businesses.

Why are Google Plus Local reviews important for SEO?

The hosts state that G Plus Local reviews are valuable for local SEO. They encourage listeners to visit their G Plus Local page, add the business to a circle, give a plus one, and leave a review, treating it as a meaningful ranking and reputation signal.

What should local restaurant websites include to rank better in mobile search?

The episode advises local restaurants to post easily accessible hours of operation, a menu with cuisine descriptions, and pricing. Doing so helps the business appear in Google's filtered Android search results and attracts customers who already understand what to expect.

What is the branding tip from episode 233 of the SEO podcast?

The tip carried over from episode 233 is to maintain consistent branding across all platforms. This means keeping the same logo, color scheme, and communication language everywhere, similar to how major brands like McDonald's and Nike maintain uniform visual identity.
