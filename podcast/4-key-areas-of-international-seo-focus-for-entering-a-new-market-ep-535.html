International SEO requires four focused actions: building country-coded URL structures, optimizing page experience for local users, researching country-specific keywords, and earning backlinks from country-specific domains.

Key takeaways
  • Use a country-coded URL structure like .uk or subfolder /uk to signal Google which audience each page targets.
  • Translate content, display local currency, and add href lang tags so international users get a friction-free on-site experience.
  • Backlinks from country-specific domains signal trustworthiness to Google; irrelevant foreign links can look like manipulation.
  • Proper upfront planning shortens overall SEO timelines and reduces costly rework when entering a new market.

Questions this episode answers

What are href lang tags and why do they matter for international SEO?

Href lang tags are signals that tell Google the language used on a page. Google then shows that version to users from IP addresses that speak that language. You can also target specific countries, for example DE for Germany and DE-CH for German-speaking Switzerland.

Do keyword differences really matter between countries that speak the same language?

Yes. Even within the same language, terms differ by country. The episode uses elevator versus lift as an example of a US versus UK difference. Tools like SEMrush and Ahrefs can help research search volumes and the correct terms for each target country.

Terms defined in this episode
ccTLD
A country-code top-level domain such as .uk or .co.uk used to signal to Google which country a website or page is intended for.
href lang tag
An HTML attribute that tells Google the language and optionally the country a page targets, so Google shows it to users from matching IP addresses.
subfolder
A URL structure such as yourdomain.com/uk that organizes international pages under the main domain, keeping all organic traffic data in one place.
country manager
A person native to or deeply familiar with a target country who helps ensure local dialects, nuances, and messaging are handled correctly for that market.
