Questions this episode answers

What is review leakage and why does it hurt a business?

Review leakage happens when customers who could leave a review do not. Each missed review reduces a Google Business Profile's power because Google treats reviews as a proxy for popularity. Leakage also occurs when prospective customers read reviews and choose a competitor, costing the business a conversion it could have won.

How does AI improve the way businesses respond to Google reviews?

Write Response AI builds a fact library from a client's website and past reviews, then uses an agentic flow to match incoming reviews to relevant facts. Responses include specific products or services the reviewer may not know about, giving prospective customers and LLMs useful information rather than a generic acknowledgment.

Why do review responses matter for AI search and LLM visibility?

LLMs like Perplexity read both reviews and responses when evaluating a business. Generic responses that only repeat the review add no value. Responses that include real business facts give AI tools substantive content to cite, improving how the business appears in AI-generated answers.

What is a practical tip for small businesses to get more reviews?

George Swetlitz recommends pulling key variables from a CRM, such as case type, staff name, or outcome, into a personalized, emotionally resonant review request. Even a simple Google Sheet with those variables can double or triple review conversion rates without requiring a full CRM integration.

Terms defined in this episode
Review leakage
Lost review opportunities that reduce a Google Business Profile's authority, including customers who never leave a review and prospective buyers who read reviews and choose a competitor instead.
Agentic flow
A series of narrow, task-specific AI agents that each handle one decision or evaluation step in sequence, producing more accurate outputs than a single broad prompt.
PHI (Personal Health Information)
Personal health data identified by a dedicated agent in the review-response workflow to ensure nothing in violation of HIPAA appears in a published response.
Fact library
A curated set of business-specific details, built from a client's website and past reviews, that an AI draws on to write review responses containing useful marketing information.
