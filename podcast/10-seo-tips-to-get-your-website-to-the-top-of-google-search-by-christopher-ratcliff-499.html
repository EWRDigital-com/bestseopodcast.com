Questions this episode answers

What is RankBrain and how should I optimize for it?

RankBrain is Google's machine learning artificial intelligence system. It activates especially for long-tail and brand-new queries. The recommended approach is to create content that answers unique queries particularly relevant to your audience's personas, rather than targeting only short seed keywords.

How important are Google reviews for local SEO?

Reviews are described as a cornerstone of local SEO. The data referenced in the episode suggests getting to at least 12 reviews. Higher star ratings and more visible customer reviews directly improve local rankings, since people use reviews to crowdsource information in the social economy.

Should I use paid ads instead of SEO to rank on Google?

Paid ads can complement SEO, but the hosts caution that many markets have been bid up to the point where ROI is no longer feasible. There is also more user trust associated with organic SEO results, and younger audiences show increasing mistrust of paid ads.

How do I speed up my website for better SEO?

The episode recommends optimizing images, fixing redirects, using caching, checking for hacked pages that inflate sitemaps, and considering AMP pages for mobile speed. Hosts note that about 80 percent of speed improvements are not technically difficult and can be done without advanced server coding.

Terms defined in this episode
RankBrain
Google's machine learning artificial intelligence system that helps interpret long-tail and never-before-seen search queries.
Micro moments
Mobile search opportunities, discussed as early as 2016, where users look for nearby businesses or services. Optimization focuses on anticipation, relevance, and ease of use.
AMP pages
Accelerated Mobile Pages, a format that speeds up mobile page loads. The hosts include it on their optimization checklist and note it is still relevant despite early predictions it might disappear.
Knowledge graph
A Google feature that surfaces direct answers, also called position zero or a rich snippet. Earning placement there is described as insanely valuable.
