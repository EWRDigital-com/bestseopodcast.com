Key takeaways
  • Companies using video get 41% more web traffic from search than those not using video.
  • Including a video on a landing page can increase conversion rates by 80%, per Unbounce data.
  • Video in email leads to 200 to 300% increase in click-through rates according to HubSpot.
  • Social video generates 1,200% more shares than text and images combined.

17 Stats And Facts Every Marketer Should Know About Video MarketingLeveraging video marketing is a powerful strategy to promote, brand, and grow your business online. More consumers are interacting with videos today than ever before and, in 2019, it’s vital to boost and ramp up your video marketing to connect with your audience. Here are 17 intriguing statistics that illustrates the power behind video marketing.

Questions this episode answers

How much does video on a landing page improve conversions?

According to Unbounce data cited in this episode, including a video on a landing page can increase conversion rates by 80%. The hosts call it one of the most impactful single changes you can make to a landing page, especially when combined with testimonials.

How does video in email affect click-through rates?

The episode cites HubSpot data showing video in email leads to a 200 to 300% increase in click-through rates. The hosts also note that embedded video inside the email, rather than just a link, tends to perform better because audiences are skeptical of external links.
