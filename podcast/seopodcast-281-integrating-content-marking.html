#SEOpodcast 281 - Integrating Content Marking

Episode36 min2015-08-17

Content marketing works best when you build the right team, set measurable goals, align all marketing channels, and prioritize quality over quantity to drive real audience action.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Create content around what prospects are actually searching for to improve ranking chances.
  • A content team needs writers, seeders, editors, data analysts, and outreach specialists to succeed.
  • Track content results beyond views; measure newsletter signups, shares, or calls to gauge real impact.
  • Sync content marketing with all other channels, including email, social, and paid, toward the same business goals.
  • Help prospects first and sell second; freely given value builds trust and encourages future business.

Terms defined in this episode
Seeders
People with influential social connections and followers who can share new content so it spreads faster across relevant niches of social networks.
KPIs in content marketing
A new set of success metrics tied to specific business goals, such as shares, follows, sign-ups, or calls, used to evaluate whether content is performing.
Remarketing
A campaign that serves ads to prospects already in the sales funnel, for example chasing someone sitting on a proposal to push them back down the sales process.
Panda algorithm
A Google algorithm referenced in the episode that can penalize sites for low-quality or thin content.
