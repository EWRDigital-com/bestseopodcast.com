Questions this episode answers

Why is human oversight important when using AI tools for paid advertising?

AI ad platforms optimize against whatever data and settings they are given. Without a human strategist ensuring clean tracking data, correct bid structures, and proper campaign settings, the algorithm compounds small problems into larger ones and spends budget on unqualified traffic or irrelevant search terms, making performance worse over time.

How long does it take for a full-funnel paid media campaign to show results?

Anthony recommends committing to at least three to six months, ideally a full year. New ad accounts need a learning phase of about a week, a first month of data before meaningful optimization, and for upper-funnel brand campaigns, at least six months to a year before revenue impact becomes clear.

What is the Warc multiplier effect study and what does it show?

The multiplier effect is a study from Warc, described as an industry advertising association, comparing brands that invested heavily in upper-funnel brand campaigns versus those focused only on lower-funnel performance. Brands taking a balanced approach, with 50% or more in brand campaigns, achieved 10x revenue over five years compared to purely transactional advertisers.

What common paid media mistakes do agencies most often find when auditing brand accounts?

The most frequent issues include missing remarketing campaigns, no target CPA set in bidding settings even on accounts spending 200K a month, and AI Max or Advantage Plus settings triggering hundreds of irrelevant search terms or expanding audiences beyond the intended target, leading to high CPAs and unqualified traffic.

Terms defined in this episode
GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
The practice of optimizing content so it is featured in AI-powered generative search engines, mentioned alongside traditional SEO as a strategy brands should embrace.
Day Parting
Scheduling ads to run at specific times of day across different platforms, such as audio ads during morning commutes and CTV ads in the evening, to match the audience mindset and location.
UGC (User Generated Content) style ads
Branded short-form videos created by independent content creators in a self-shot, authentic style, used in paid ad campaigns without requiring creators to have large followings.
Influenced Revenue
An attribution method that tracks users exposed to an upper-funnel ad who later visited the website and made a purchase within a set window, such as 30 days, to show upper-funnel campaign value.
