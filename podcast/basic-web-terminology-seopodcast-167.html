Questions this episode answers

What is pay-per-click advertising and how does it work?

Pay-per-click is a form of paid search where advertisers bid on key phrases. You only pay when someone clicks your ad, not when they see it. A call tracking number placed in the ad can generate phone calls even from people who never click, resulting in zero cost for those leads.

What is conversion rate optimization and why does it matter for paid search?

Conversion rate optimization, called CRO or CVO in the episode, is the process of guiding site visitors to take a desired action such as calling, emailing, downloading, or subscribing. For paid search it is especially important because you can stop, adjust, and restart campaigns based on which actions produce results.

What is split testing and when should it be used in paid search?

Split testing, also called A and B testing, means running two different ads, calls to action, or landing pages simultaneously to see which performs better. After enough data is gathered you choose the best option. The hosts apply it mainly to larger accounts or smaller accounts where clients are willing to pay for the extra work.

How can scheduling and geotargeting improve a PPC campaign on a low budget?

Scheduling lets you run ads only during hours when you can respond to inquiries, so you avoid paying for clicks you cannot convert. Geotargeting focuses spend on the areas where you are most profitable. Together they stretch a limited budget by eliminating wasted impressions and clicks.

Terms defined in this episode
Pay-Per-Click (PPC)
A form of paid search where advertisers bid on key phrases and pay only when a user clicks the ad, not when the ad is viewed.
Conversion
The action a visitor takes after landing on a site, such as calling, filling out a form, downloading something, or subscribing, and in paid search can also refer to a click-through from an impression.
Split Testing
Running two different ads, calls to action, or landing pages simultaneously to gather data and determine which version drives the desired action better. Also called A and B testing.
Geotargeting
A PPC campaign setting that limits ad delivery to geographic areas where the advertiser is most profitable, helping maximize a limited budget.
