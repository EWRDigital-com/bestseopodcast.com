Questions this episode answers

Why should my website use HTTPS in 2017?

Starting January 2017, Chrome marks sites without HTTPS as non-secure when they collect sensitive information like passwords or credit card details. This warning discourages users from proceeding, which can hurt conversions. Sites that allow logins or sell products need to be on a secure server.

How should I promote a blog post after publishing it?

Include an excerpt with a read-more link in your newsletter, tweet the post, summarize it with a link on Facebook, and create a meme using the featured image for Instagram. The core idea is that content will not attract visitors on its own without active promotion across multiple channels.

What is the benefit of running both PPC and organic search listings at the same time?

When a pay-per-click ad appears at the top of the page alongside a well-placed organic listing, the click-through rate on the organic result increases. The hosts attribute this to credibility: users see the brand twice and perceive it as more trustworthy, making them more likely to click.

How can I fix pages that rank well but get low engagement?

Identify pages that rank highly but show low activity in analytics, then improve their CTAs, layout, and functionality. Also link from those well-ranking pages to other pages you want to rank better, passing link juice to underperforming pages and improving overall SEO engagement.

Terms defined in this episode
Shiko
An eWebResults branded term for social engagement, standing for shares, likes, and follows across social media platforms.
Evergreen content
Content that is valuable today and in the future, but should be updated periodically as technology and algorithms change.
Contextual content
Writing that uses varied related terms and phrases for a concept rather than repeating one keyword, reflecting how people naturally speak and search.
