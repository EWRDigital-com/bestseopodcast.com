Questions this episode answers

How do I find out who my real online competitors are using SpyFu?

Type your domain into SpyFu's domain overview tool and scroll down to the organic and paid competitors section. The businesses that appear there may differ from the companies you think of as rivals, and competitors not top of mind could be bigger threats than you first thought.

Can SpyFu show competitor ranking history even if I never tracked them before?

Yes. SpyFu stores a large collection of historical data, so you can review a website's past rankings without having previously set up a campaign to track it. This lets you spot past SEO successes or declines and understand what drove them.

How should I interpret the traffic and budget numbers SpyFu provides?

Treat them as estimations and focus on relative differences. If SpyFu says you spend five thousand and a competitor spends ten, the competitor is likely spending proportionally more. The relative comparison is what guides strategic decisions, not the raw figures.

What tools besides SpyFu are mentioned for competitor analysis?

The episode mentions Google Alerts for brand mentions, Built With for identifying competitor technology stacks, Similar Web for traffic and referral data, Quantcast for audience demographics, BuzzSumo for most-shared content, and What Runs Where and AdBeat for tracking competitor display ads.

Terms defined in this episode
Domain Overview Tool
A SpyFu feature that lets you quickly identify competitors and review ratios of SEO versus PPC traffic, core keywords, and keywords a competitor ranks or bids on that you do not.
Backlink Profile
The collection of inbound links pointing to a website, which reveals how competitors earn authority and which site owners and authors you might also approach for links.
Competitive Intelligence
The practice of monitoring and analyzing competitor marketing strategies, keywords, ads, and content to inform your own marketing decisions.
Top Pages Feature
A SpyFu tool that shows which pages on a competitor's site bring them the most traffic from search engine result pages.
