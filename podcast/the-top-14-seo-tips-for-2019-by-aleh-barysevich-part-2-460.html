Questions this episode answers

Are backlinks still important for SEO in 2019?

Yes. Backlinks remain the strongest indication of authority to Google. Quality matters more than quantity, and links from a single domain carry much less weight than links from many different domains. Regular backlink audits are recommended to catch and disavow spammy links.

What is the best tool to check page speed for SEO?

The hosts recommend Google Lighthouse over Page Speed Insights, saying Lighthouse data is more accurate. It is built into Chrome and can be accessed by right-clicking a page, selecting inspect, and clicking the audit tab to fire the Lighthouse audit tool.

How important is HTTPS for SEO rankings?

HTTPS is a confirmed ranking signal and a trust factor for users. The hosts share a case where a startup lost an investor because their site lacked the lock symbol. Any site collecting user information or doing e-commerce especially needs to be secured.

How do you increase dwell time on a page?

The hosts suggest adding video content to encourage visitors to stay longer after getting their initial answer, and adding a comment section so users spend more time engaging. They target a session duration of two minutes and a bounce rate at or below 60 percent.

Terms defined in this episode
SEO Siloing
An internal link structure strategy where pages are organized and linked to signal topical relevance and page authority within a website, helping pages rank without relying solely on external backlinks.
Anchor Text Diversity
Using a variety of anchor text phrases when building links so that no single keyword phrase is repeated excessively, which helps avoid algorithmic or manual penalties.
Lighthouse
A Google audit tool built into the Chrome browser that the hosts recommend over Page Speed Insights for more accurate page performance and SEO data.
Link Authority
The quality and trustworthiness of websites linking to your site. High authority links carry more ranking value, and spammy or low quality links can be disavowed through Google Search Console.
