Questions this episode answers

What are the most important on-page SEO factors according to this podcast?

The hosts say to focus on three things: content, HTML code, and site architecture. Content drives search engines and site visitors, architecture guides navigation, and clean compliant code helps pages load faster and rank better. They call these the three core things for on-page SEO.

Which social media platform is growing fastest among older users?

According to statistics cited in the episode, the fastest growing demographic on Twitter is the 55 to 64 year age bracket. The hosts believe this is because baby boomers and business owners are being pushed into social media marketing and creating Twitter profiles for their businesses.

Do Google Plus plus ones affect SEO rankings?

The hosts believe the Google Plus one button has an indirect effect on SEO rankings, comparing it to a Facebook like, a Twitter follower, or even a backlink. They suggest that a plus one from a high-authority person like Danny Sullivan could carry more value than a link from a major site.
