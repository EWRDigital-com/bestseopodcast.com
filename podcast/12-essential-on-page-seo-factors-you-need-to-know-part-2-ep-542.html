Questions this episode answers

Why are inbound backlinks more important than internal or outbound links for SEO?

Inbound links, also called backlinks, provide the biggest SEO benefit because they act as votes of trust from other domains. However, links from link farms, forum posts, or guest books can be spammy and actually tank rankings if not disavowed. Quality and relevance of the linking source matter greatly.

How does Google My Business differ from regular national SEO?

According to this episode, Google My Business is a separate platform with a similar but distinct algorithm from national search. Factors like accurate hours, uploaded images, filled-out services, and NAP citation consistency all affect local map rankings differently than they would affect organic national search rankings.

What is the biggest cause of slow site speed?

The most common culprit is images that are uploaded improperly, in the wrong format, or too large in file size. The hosts estimate that seven out of ten sites have this problem. Enabling compression, reducing redirects, and using content delivery networks are recommended fixes.

How important are keywords in URL structure for SEO?

URL keywords play a much smaller role than they once did, but they still may influence a site's initial ranking and help Google group pages. Keeping URL structures simple, consistent, and logically organized across the site builds crawlability and trust with Google's algorithm.

Terms defined in this episode
NAP
Name, address, and phone number. Must be identical across all local listings and citations to ensure continuity for local SEO.
Inbound links
Links from other websites pointing to your page, also called backlinks. Of the three link types, these provide the biggest SEO benefit but must come from reputable sources.
Google Core Web Vitals
Google's minimum threshold for site performance, including page speed and visual stability, that websites must meet to satisfy current SEO requirements.
Link farms
Networks of low-quality or fake links intended to cheat the ranking system. Links from link farms can tank rankings if not disavowed.
