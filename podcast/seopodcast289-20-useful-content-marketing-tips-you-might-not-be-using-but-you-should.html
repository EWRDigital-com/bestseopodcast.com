Questions this episode answers

How should I find blog topic ideas for my business?

Check your email inbox for repeat customer questions and ask your sales and customer service teams about common objections or questions. If those questions keep coming up, there is likely no content on your site addressing them, so writing blog posts on those topics helps both customers and your team.

Why should I embed videos in blog posts?

Blogs with videos are more likely to get additional hits and shares than those without. Before embedding, optimize the video on YouTube by giving it a strong title, a lengthy keyword-rich description, and voiceover so Google can transcribe it, which adds to its ranking value.

What does putting promotion first mean in content marketing?

Before developing content, decide where it will be posted, such as your blog, Pinterest, SlideShare, or social channels. Then have all supporting assets, like featured images sized for Instagram or Facebook, created at the same time so you can promote effectively the moment the content goes live.

How do outbound links in a fact list help your content?

When you link facts in your content to credible external sources like a Forbes article, reader trust in your site increases. From a Google perspective it signals a quality outbound link, and if that external site has alerts set up, they may link back to you in return.

A Google feature that displays direct answers, such as health condition explanations, right in search results, reducing clicks to external sites and already linked to a reported 40 percent traffic decrease for some content publishers.
Platform-specific CTA
A call to action in a social media bio customized to each platform, for example using the word subscribe on YouTube or follow on Twitter, rather than using the same generic bio everywhere.
Who-to-follow list
A piece of content that compiles non-competitor industry contacts worth following, which positions you as a trusted source and encourages those listed to link back to you.
