Questions this episode answers

What is CIVO and how does it differ from conversion optimization?

CIVO stands for Search Engine Visitor Optimization and is the term used by eWebStyle for what the broader industry calls conversion optimization. It focuses on what you do with a visitor after they arrive on your website from a search engine. The hosts say they prefer the CIVO label.

Should a service business show prices on its website?

According to the hosts, it depends on the industry. For customized services where cost can range widely, listing a broad price range is more likely to scare away potential clients than to reassure them. Showing prices is most important for e-commerce product pages.

Why are anchor text links in body content risky after Penguin updates?

The hosts explain that Penguin and Panda updates flag pages that have large numbers of links sharing the same keyword anchor text as spammy. They recommend treading softly with keyword-rich anchor text and note that generic phrases like click here have regained some value with Google.

What are the top conversion optimization tips covered in this episode?

The episode covers ten tips from a Search Engine Journal article: make your phone number visible, answer emails and calls, fix typos, show prices and shipping info, create an About Us page, underline links, create a custom 404 error page, add links to body content, fix broken links, and include calls to action on every page.

Terms defined in this episode
CIVO (Search Engine Visitor Optimization)
An eWebStyle term for optimizing what happens to a visitor after they arrive on your website from a search engine, equivalent to the industry term conversion optimization.
Custom 404 Error Page
A branded error page that keeps your site navigation intact when a visitor lands on a missing page, preventing a total bounce by letting them find other content.
Penguin 1.1
A data update within the Penguin algorithm module that used secret signals to affect how search results are displayed, described in the episode as a refresh inside an existing algorithm module.
Anchor Text
The clickable, visible words used in a hyperlink. Keyword-rich anchor text repeated too many times across a site can be flagged as spammy by Panda and Penguin updates.
