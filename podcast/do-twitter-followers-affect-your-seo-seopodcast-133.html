Questions this episode answers

Do Twitter followers affect your SEO?

According to Google and Bing, the authority of the person sharing a link on Twitter does affect the weight of that link. More authoritative Twitter accounts pass more link weight, though Google specifies this is independent of PageRank. So follower authority matters, but not as a direct PageRank signal.

Does social media buzz affect regular search results?

Yes. Both Google and Bing confirmed that social media buzz is used as a ranking signal for regular search results, especially for news. Real-time and social search results are described as heavily affected, with authority metrics used to determine which posts are hot.

Do Twitter links pass link juice to your website?

Google says Twitter link data is used in some limited situations. Bing says it depends on how often a link is posted, the number of tweets and retweets, and the authority of the people posting it. Using URL shorteners may reduce the link value passed.

Why do meta descriptions matter for SEO beyond click-through rates?

Meta descriptions can appear as the text shown under site link snippets in Google search results. The hosts cite the example of a site where meta description text showed up directly under each of the six site links displayed, making it the first opportunity to sell to a searcher.

Terms defined in this episode
Author Authority
A measure of how influential or popular a social media profile is, which affects the link weight passed when that user shares a URL on Twitter or Facebook, according to both Google and Bing.
Site Link Snippets
Additional links shown beneath a main search result, where the accompanying text is pulled from the page's meta description.
SEVO
Search Engine Visitor Optimization, a concept the hosts describe as starting at the search engine result page, focusing on optimizing what searchers see before they click.
Search Plus
Google's feature that incorporates Google Plus content into search results, which Twitter criticized for omitting Twitter results from those personalized results.
