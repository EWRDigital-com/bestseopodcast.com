Questions this episode answers

How did Houston Real Producers grow its online mastermind attendance so fast?

Taylor Waidhofer reports attendance grew from 150 at the first Zoom mastermind to 275 the second month, then 1300 the third month, over 600 the fourth month, and over 770 at the fifth session, driven by putting out strong value and letting word spread.

What is the Houston Real Producers business model?

The platform is free for real estate agents. Vendors such as builders, lenders, title companies, and roofers pay to reach those agents. Houston Real Producers showcases the top 500 agents on LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, in print, and at events, acting as a master connector for the Houston real estate community.

How can someone use social media to make referrals quickly?

Taylor Waidhofer recommends posting on LinkedIn or Facebook stating exactly what your contact is looking for, tagging that person, then watching the comments. He says the person needing the referral can take over the conversation from there, making the process fast and simple.

What content strategy does Taylor Waidhofer use for his daily video series?

Taylor produces a 45 to 60 second daily video called the Daily Disruption for LinkedIn and Facebook. He finds a topic from everyday life, sometimes involves his kids, and says he is pushing close to 500 episodes. His producer Blaine Walker with Tiny Sparrow Marketing helped launch the concept.
