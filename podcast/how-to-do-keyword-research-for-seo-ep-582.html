How to Do Keyword Research for SEO Ep. 582

Matthew Bertram covers keyword research and its key role in shaping a successful SEO strategy. See how understanding what your audience wants and aligning them with your business objectives can lead to content that matters.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Start from search intent and topics, not single keywords. Figure out what the searcher is actually trying to do before you target a term.
  • The exact keyword you want to rank for is rarely the most important target. Build content around the whole topic, not one phrase.
  • Use keyword tools to triangulate direction, but treat their traffic estimates as rough signals, not hard numbers.
  • Audit your own site and the keywords you already rank for before chasing brand-new ones.
  • If you use AI to draft content, disclose it and add real expert opinion, or it reads as thin and undifferentiated.
Understand what the topic is first, not just the keyword.
