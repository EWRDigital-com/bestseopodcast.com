Questions this episode answers

What is a trip wire in a sales funnel?

A trip wire is a low-price entry offer relevant to your overall price point. At eWebResults it is a 499 dollar from problems to profit plan. It is 100 percent refundable, and the fee applies toward future services. The goal is to get prospects to open their wallets and see themselves as customers.

How many form fields should a lead capture form have?

Keep forms to three or four fields. Each additional form field drops conversions by about 17 percent. eWebResults asks for name, email, and phone as required fields, with website listed as optional, and recommends collecting extra information during the follow-up phone call instead.

Why should you deliver a proposal live instead of emailing it?

Emailing a proposal makes it easy for prospects to compare it out of context against competitors. Delivering it live, by phone or in person, lets you walk through a custom plan, address questions in real time, and frame it as an action plan rather than a generic proposal, which improves conversions.

What is an easy ask and why does it matter for conversions?

An easy ask is a lead magnet, such as a white paper, industry stats, or a free analysis, offered in exchange for a name, email, and phone number. It starts the sales process, establishes expertise, and gets prospects to raise their hands without a heavy commitment. Creative naming, like a free comfort analysis instead of free consultation, sparks curiosity.

Terms defined in this episode
Trip Wire
A low-price entry offer designed to convert a prospect into a paying customer. At eWebResults it is priced at 499 dollars, is 100 percent refundable, and the fee is applied toward chosen services if the prospect moves forward.
Risk Reversal
A sales tactic where the business takes on the risk by offering a money-back guarantee, so the customer keeps all deliverables even if unsatisfied, building trust and reducing hesitation.
Easy Ask
A lead magnet such as a white paper, coupon, or free analysis offered to website visitors in exchange for contact information, designed to begin the sales funnel with minimal friction.
BS Filter
An alternate term for a trip wire. A small required payment that qualifies prospects by separating serious buyers from tire kickers before a full sales conversation begins.
