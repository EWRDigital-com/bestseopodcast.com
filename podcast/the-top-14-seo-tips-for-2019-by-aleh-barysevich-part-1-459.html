Questions this episode answers

What are the top SEO ranking signals to optimize for in 2019 according to Aleh Barysevich?

The article covers 14 signals grouped into categories including relevance and content. The first seven discussed are: accordance to search intent, CTR, keywords on the page, grammar, well-structured HTML, schema markup, and content uniqueness. Quality content that comprehensively answers user questions is the foundation for all of them.

How does search intent affect SEO performance?

About 80% of searchers are looking for information rather than ready to buy. Google has largely figured out intent for most queries and shows results accordingly. If the results on a SERP do not match your expected audience, that keyword likely has the wrong intent for your campaign, and targeting it wastes effort.

Why does duplicate content hurt SEO and how can you fix it?

Google penalizes duplication and rewards uniqueness. Online stores often copy manufacturer product descriptions, which are shared across many sites. Writing unique product descriptions, adding original images, testimonials, and encouraging customer reviews on product pages can differentiate a site and improve rankings.

How can you improve click-through rate from search results pages?

Pay close attention to meta descriptions on pages that rank well but have low CTR, as title tags and meta descriptions may not be relevant or enticing enough. A-B testing ad copy in AdWords lets you gather data quickly to find which messaging drives clicks before applying it to your SEO pages.

Terms defined in this episode
Search Intent
The underlying goal a user has when performing a search. Roughly 80% of searches are informational, meaning users seek research rather than an immediate purchase.
TF-IDF Analysis
A method that calculates how frequently certain keywords appear on competitor pages ranking for a given query, providing data on competitive keyword density.
Schema Markup
Structured data added to HTML that helps search engines categorize and understand page content. Google provides testing tools and guidelines for implementing it correctly.
LSI Graph
A tool used to find latent semantic indexing related terms that should accompany a primary keyword on a page to signal topical comprehensiveness to Google.
