Content Channel Efficiency - #SEPodcast 233

Episode31 min2014-06-11

Content channel efficiency requires consistent branding, aligned messaging, analytics review, and updated content across all channels, as outlined in eight steps from a Marketing Land article discussed in episode 233.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Keep branding visually identical across every platform, including logo, colors, and proportions.
  • Align all content messaging with buyer personas so it speaks the language of your specific audience.
  • Find pages with high bounce rates and low conversions, then adjust content format or add prominent calls to action.
  • Remove or repurpose outdated content like past events and old seasonal promos to keep your brand current.
  • Constantly upgrade content production tools and formats so your output improves alongside your audience's changing devices.

Questions this episode answers

What are content channels and why should they work together?

Content channels are different ways you put out content, including your website, blog, video, social media, email, direct mail, TV, and radio. They should work together so your branding, messaging, and promotions stay consistent and reinforce each other across every platform.

How should I handle pages with high bounce rates in analytics?

Find pages with both high page views and high bounce rates. If the page has no conversions, make changes like converting content to video, breaking it into bullet points, or adding a more prominent call to action. If conversions are already high, leave the page alone.

What is the double Irish tax strategy mentioned in the episode?

The double Irish is a tax avoidance process where companies funnel contracts through Ireland because it has the lowest corporate tax structure. The episode notes Google reported 12.4 billion in Ireland sales versus 138 million in France, and France hit Google with a large tax bill over this practice.

Should I switch from All in One SEO to Yoast for WordPress authorship?

Switching plugins is relatively easy since you just install Yoast and it takes over, but moving custom title and meta description content between the two takes extra work. If you are comfortable with All in One SEO, you can manually set up Google authorship by editing your Google Plus About page and adding the rel author tag without switching plugins.

Terms defined in this episode
Content channel
A way of distributing content, including a website, blog, video, social media, email, direct mail, TV, or radio.
Positioning vs. ranking
Positioning refers to whether your site appears in a top spot across local, paid, or organic listings, while ranking is a single numeric measure that changes based on IP address, login status, and personalization.
rel=author
An HTML link attribute added to pages that connects content back to a Google Plus profile to establish authorship.
Double Irish
A tax avoidance process where companies funnel contracts through Ireland because it has the lowest corporate tax structure, allowing them to report far lower revenue in higher-tax countries.
