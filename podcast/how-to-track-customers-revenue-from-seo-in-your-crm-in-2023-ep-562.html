Questions this episode answers

How do you connect SEO attribution data to a CRM?

Add hidden fields to your website lead generation forms and use a tool like Attributor.io to write the source channel and campaign data into those fields before the form is submitted. That data then flows into the CRM alongside the lead's name, email, and phone number.

What CRM reports should SEO professionals run to prove SEO value?

The episode recommends five reports: number of leads from SEO versus other channels, number of customers from SEO, revenue from SEO by landing page group, average deal size from SEO versus other channels, and time to close from SEO versus other channels.

Is SEO cheaper to acquire customers than Google Ads or Facebook Ads?

In the example discussed in the episode, SEO acquisition cost was $200 per customer, compared to $416 for Google Ads and $714 for Facebook Ads, and average customer value was higher for SEO at $1,800 versus $1,500 for Google Ads and $1,142 for Facebook Ads.

Why does time to close matter when reporting on SEO performance?

Knowing the time to close helps set accurate expectations for when SEO budget increases will show up in revenue. It also prevents finance teams from pulling the budget too early if results have not yet materialized, because SEO programs often need six months to a year to gain traction.

Terms defined in this episode
Hidden form fields
Fields added to website lead generation forms that capture attribution data such as source channel and campaign, then pass that data invisibly into a CRM when the form is submitted.
Time to close
The metric measuring how long it takes a lead or visitor to convert into a paying customer, useful for modeling when SEO budget changes will impact revenue.
Attribution
The process of identifying which marketing channel, campaign, or touchpoint was responsible for generating a lead or customer, used to measure SEO and paid channel performance.
Looker Studio
A third-party analytics reporting tool, formerly called Google Data Studios, that can connect to CRM data for advanced SEO reporting.
