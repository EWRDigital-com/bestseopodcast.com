Do Not Underestimate the Power of YouTube by Jim Yu #298

Ep. 29829 min2015-11-10

YouTube video optimization boosts brand reach and search rankings by focusing on watch time, keyword relevance, subscriber count, strong calls to action, and Google's audio transcription of video content.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Keep YouTube videos under three minutes to maintain high watch time, the top ranking signal.
  • Optimize video titles, tags, and descriptions with the same keywords used on your related site pages.
  • Sync your Google Plus local business profile with your YouTube channel to enable annotations that link to your website.
  • Embed videos on your site page and promote the page link, not the direct video link, to drive site traffic.
  • Speak your target keywords in the first few sentences of your video because Google transcribes audio content.
having a strong YouTube presence can have an incredible impact on your brand reach and will give you the chance to engage with the millions of visitors using the platform

About this episode

Billions of video views take place on YouTube every day. Is your brand capturing any of this traffic? Tune in as Chris and Charles share some tips on how you can optimize your videos for maximum exposure.

Questions this episode answers

What is the most important ranking factor for YouTube videos?

Watch time is the most important algorithm indicator for YouTube video ranking. It measures how long viewers watch your video, whether they complete it or drop off early. Keeping videos under three minutes and auto-playing them on pages can help increase watch time.

How should you optimize a YouTube video for search?

Include your focus keyword in the title, use a small number of tightly focused tags, write a full keyword-optimized description, add a transcript or closed captions, and speak your target keywords within the first few sentences of the video since Google transcribes the audio.

What types of video content perform best on YouTube?

Jim Yu recommends emotional and educational videos. The hosts add entertaining content as a third category. Videos that are overly promotional or irrelevant to a search query tend to fail. Content should also be relevant, up to date, and original, similar to written content.

How can YouTube annotations help drive traffic to your website?

By syncing a verified Google Plus local business profile with your YouTube channel, you can create annotations that link directly to pages on your website. These annotation links remain functional even when the video is embedded on other sites or shared on social platforms.

In their words

YouTube users are not specifically here to learn about your brand. They want to see videos relevant to their search query.

Guest
Terms defined in this episode
Watch Time
The measure of how long viewers watch a YouTube video, described in this episode as the most important algorithm indicator for YouTube video ranking.
YouTube Annotations
Clickable overlays added to YouTube videos that can link to other videos, a Google Plus page, or, when a verified Google Plus profile is synced, directly to pages on your own website.
Forced App Download Redirect
An automatic redirect that sends mobile visitors to download an app instead of showing the website, penalized by Google as of November 3rd according to this episode.
Closed Captioning
Text captions added to a video that help YouTube and Google understand video content and can improve ranking by providing readable, keyword-relevant text.
