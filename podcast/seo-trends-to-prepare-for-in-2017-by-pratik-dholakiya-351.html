Questions this episode answers

Why does voice search matter for SEO in 2017?

Voice search is one of the fastest growing search options, driven by devices like Alexa and Google Home. Users ask full conversational questions rather than short keyword phrases, so site content must be semantic and contextual to match those queries and get found.

What does optimizing for user intent mean in practice?

It means studying what search terms bring visitors to your site, which pages they view most, and how long they stay, then creating content that matches what users are actually looking for rather than what you assume or want them to see.

Terms defined in this episode
AMP pages
Accelerated mobile pages made specifically for ranking fast and being user-friendly on mobile devices, stripping heavy background code and images to focus on content.
Schema markup
Code added to a site that tells Google exactly what specific content is, increasing the chance that content appears as a rich snippet or in the knowledge graph.
Cross-channel marketing
Using several channels such as paid ads, social campaigns, and optimized web pages in an integrated way to market the same brand and product to the same target audience.
