About this episode

For this weeks podcast, we go over a few tips on how to re-evaluate SEO strategies to regain lost ranking from algorithm updates. Afterwords, we go over google+, and the many SEO opportunities that it has including the social media side and the author tag/pages.

Questions this episode answers

How can Google Plus help my website rank in search results?

According to this episode, you can embed anchor text links in your Google Plus profile description to pass SEO juice, optimize post titles as title tags, post new content to get it indexed faster, and connect with influencers in your industry to build trust and authority for your site.

What is the Google Plus author tag and why does it matter for SEO?

The author tag displays your Google Plus profile avatar next to your content in Google search results. The episode recommends using a professional headshot so viewers recognize you, because the author tag is meant to build relevancy and establish personal branding in search listings.

Is it too late to start using Google Plus for SEO?

The hosts say it is not too late. They note that only one in four people log into Google Plus once a month, meaning early adopters still have an advantage. They recommend dedicating the same time to Google Plus that you already give to Facebook and Twitter.

Terms defined in this episode
EMD update
An Google algorithm update targeting exact match domain names, specifically aimed at spam and low quality sites that happen to have a keyword rich domain name, affecting about 2 percent of search queries.
Author tag
A tag that displays your Google Plus profile avatar next to your content on the Google search engine results page, used to build relevancy and establish branding for the content author.
Circle rank
A Google Plus metric explored via the tool circlecount.com that the hosts describe as likely ranking your power circle based on how many people you have in your circle and what influence they have.
Algorithm cataclysm
A recurring segment name used by the hosts to discuss major Google algorithm updates and their effects on search rankings.
