Questions this episode answers

Why is Chris Burris leaving the Best SEO Podcast?

Chris is shifting his focus to a new podcast called Live Beyond the Norm tied to his supplement company My Vital C, and to an oil and gas podcast on OGGN. He says he wants to free up Matt Berkshire to take the show in new directions without the existing format holding him back.

How should SEOs use AI tools like ChatGPT for content?

According to the episode, ChatGPT should be used as a creative aid, not a replacement for strategy. You should identify angles that have not been covered, use the tool to help assemble that unique content, and add human language throughout, because Google rewards differentiation and uniqueness above generic AI output.

What does EWR Digital say about keyword research and client strategy?

The hosts say a proper discovery process can completely change keyword targeting. In one example, spending over an hour with a client revealed that a low-volume, long-tail sub-category phrase with under 100 searches per month was the right target, rather than the obvious high-volume keyword they would have chosen using analytics alone.

What SEO changes are coming with AI according to this episode?

The hosts say the bar for content quality is rising because AI lets everyone produce decent content, making differentiation the key ranking factor. They also say search is moving toward images, many blog pages are going unindexed because content is stale, and voice search combined with YouTube is a major coming frontier.

Long-tail key phrase
A highly specific, low-search-volume search query that targets a sub-category of a sub-category, which the hosts say can convert better than broad high-volume terms.
E-E-A-T
Expertise, authoritative trust, and experience signals that Google uses to evaluate content quality; the hosts refer to it as expertise, authoritative trust, ad management, ad experience.
Token (AI content)
A unit of output from a large language model; the hosts recommend breaking up tokens and adding human language so AI-generated content does not read as purely machine-written.
