Questions this episode answers

How long do viewers watch online videos before clicking away?

According to the article discussed, 20% of viewers click away within 10 seconds, a third leave by 30 seconds, 45% by one minute, and 60% by two minutes. Mobile device users tend to watch longer, with iPad users averaging around five minutes.

Why does uploading videos to multiple hosting sites help SEO?

Nolan Davis explains that uploading the same video with matching descriptions and keywords to sites like YouTube, Dailymotion, Vimeo, and Viddler widens your footprint on the internet. YouTube also has a transcribing unit, so spoken words in the video can help with search visibility.

Terms defined in this episode
shiko
A term used on the podcast meaning a share, like, or follow on any social media platform.
evergreen content
Content that remains valuable for a long time and does not become outdated quickly, mentioned in the context of video marketing statistics articles.
top of the mind marketing
A strategy where repeated exposure to a brand or ad keeps it in a consumer's memory so they recall it when making a purchase decision.
explainer video
A video included in an email or on a website that explains a product or service, cited as increasing click-through rates by 200 to 300% in a 2010 survey.
