Questions this episode answers

How can I use SlideShare for content marketing and SEO?

Upload your presentation decks to SlideShare so users can share and reshare them to platforms like LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter. Any hot links inside your decks become clickable links on SlideShare, creating backlinks to your site that get shared along with the content.

What Google Analytics reports help you know your audience?

The demographic report shows visitors age range and gender split. The device report shows whether they use desktop, mobile, or tablet. The location report shows where they come from. Together these three reports let you build a detailed profile of who is visiting your site.

What is evergreen content and why does it matter?

Evergreen content remains relevant no matter when it is published, covering topics like how-tos and tips. It generates highly shareable content and backlinks that persist long after the article was written. Keeping it focused on your niche helps ensure it never goes out of date.

What does active reading mean as a content marketing strategy?

Active reading is the process of actively picking apart a piece of content to discern things like the narrator's perspective and unanswered questions. It lets you build knowledge, fill gaps in the market, and enhance authority by adding value and explanation beyond the original source material.
