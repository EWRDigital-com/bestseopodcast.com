Terms defined in this episode
Semantic SEO
After Google's Hummingbird update, Google understands the overall topic of a page rather than just individual keywords, so optimizing for related subtopics helps signal what a page is truly about.
LSI Graph
A tool that shows subtopics related to a keyword you type in, helping content creators cover a topic thoroughly for semantic search.
Link juice
The ranking authority passed from one page to another through internal or external links, which can be directed to boost weaker pages sitting on page two or three of Google.
Long tail keyword
A longer, more specific keyword phrase that can be embedded into title tags alongside a primary keyword to capture additional search traffic.
