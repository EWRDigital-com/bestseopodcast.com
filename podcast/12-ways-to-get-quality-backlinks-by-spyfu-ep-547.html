Questions this episode answers

What are the best white hat ways to get quality backlinks?

The episode lists 12 methods: guest blogging, asking partners or suppliers for links, claiming unlinked brand mentions, working with influencers, creating visual content, publishing original research, building free tools, writing ultimate guides, using local citations, doing press releases, running expert roundups, and mining for broken links.

How often should a business publish press releases for SEO?

According to the episode, businesses should publish press releases roughly every 28 days, which works out to about 13 press releases per year, aligning with Google News indexing cycles. Each press release is an opportunity to add at least one backlink to the site.

Are guest posts still valuable for link building even though Google discourages them?

The episode says guest posts remain valuable when the content is relevant, high quality, and written by an authority in the space. Google's concern is primarily with exchanging money for links. Writing for high authority sites like Forbes or Entrepreneur as a contributor is cited as a legitimate example.

Why are local citations important for backlink building?

Local citations on directories like Google Business Profile, Yelp, Facebook, and Apple Maps each provide a backlink and send consistency signals for name, address, and phone data that Google uses to build trust. The episode notes roughly 30% of traffic goes to Google Business Profile.

Terms defined in this episode
White hat backlinks
Links earned through legitimate, Google-approved methods that serve as votes of confidence and improve a site's prominence in search results.
Broken link building
Finding pages on other sites that link to content that no longer exists, then offering your own relevant content as a replacement to earn that backlink.
NAP listings
Citations that list a business Name, Address, and Phone number consistently across directories to build trust signals with Google.
Page rank
Google's foundational algorithm that determined a page's importance and prominence based on the quantity and quality of backlinks pointing to it.
