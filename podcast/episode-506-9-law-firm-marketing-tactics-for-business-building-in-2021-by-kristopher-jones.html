Questions this episode answers

Is PPC advertising worth it for law firms?

PPC can be very expensive in the legal industry, with high cost-per-click rates. It works best for urgent needs like DWI cases. Firms should pair it with CallRail tracking, a high-converting landing page, and remarketing where allowed, rather than treating it as a standalone solution.
