Questions this episode answers

How do you grow an email list from scratch for an e-commerce brand?

Nikita recommends starting with personal contacts from your phone or Gmail, then adding a website pop-up offering a discount like 10% off. Blog sign-up forms and giveaways are also options, though giveaway subscribers need extra nurture sequences before receiving direct sales messaging.

Why does email deliverability matter even if open rates look healthy?

A high overall open rate can mask spam placement on specific providers. Nikita warns that if Yahoo starts filtering your emails to spam, Gmail, Apple, and Verizon Media Group can follow, causing a cascading deliverability failure across your entire list.

Can ChatGPT and other LLMs actually drive sales leads for agencies or brands?

Yes. Nikita's agency at Aspect received a booked sales call sourced directly from ChatGPT. The prospect was researching email strategy, the agency appeared as an option, and the prospect booked a call. Nikita also notes that users often use LLMs to compare and validate brands they already found elsewhere.

Terms defined in this episode
LLM Visibility
The ability of a brand or agency to appear as a cited or recommended option inside large language models like ChatGPT when users search or research topics related to that brand.
Resulting
A term from the book Thinking in Bets describing the mistake of judging a decision by its outcome rather than by the quality of the decision-making framework used to make it.
Deliverability
Whether sent emails actually land in a subscriber's primary inbox versus spam folders across providers like Gmail, Yahoo, Apple, and Verizon Media Group.
Win-back flow
An automated email sequence sent to subscribers who have not opened emails in a while, designed to re-engage them before they become permanently inactive.
