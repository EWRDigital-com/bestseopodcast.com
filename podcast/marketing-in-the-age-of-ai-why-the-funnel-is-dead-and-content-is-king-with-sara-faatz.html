Questions this episode answers

Is Google dead now that AI search tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity exist?

According to Sara Faatz, Google is not dead and will not die. Search engines are evolving, not disappearing. AI is being integrated into existing search tools, and SEO remains essential. She and Matt Bertram agree that dropping SEO entirely in favor of a pure AI strategy would be a mistake.

How has the B2B customer journey changed in the age of AI?

The journey is no longer linear and now resembles a choose-your-own-adventure path. Buying groups add complexity, junior researchers often do initial vetting before executives engage, and the journey can span 18 months for complex products. AI can end the journey before a prospect ever reaches your website.

What content strategy actually works for AI discoverability?

Sara Faatz says the core tenets of solid SEO remain true for AI discoverability: create quality content with unique thought and genuine insight rather than generic AI-generated filler. Content that is concise, clear, well-structured, and uses appropriate alt text follows accessibility best practices that also make it AI friendly.

Terms defined in this episode
Zero-click search
Searches where users get the answers they need directly from AI overviews or search results without ever clicking through to a website, identified in this episode as the biggest catalyst of change in the customer journey.
AI propensity scoring
A feature within Sitefinity that uses predictive analytics to evaluate actions taken on a site and forecast a visitor's next anticipated step, enabling personalized content journeys and higher conversion rates.
Search everywhere optimization
A phrase attributed to Neil Patel in this episode, meaning marketers must optimize content for discovery across all platforms and AI tools, not just traditional search engines.
Customer Data Platform (CDP)
A platform, such as Sitefinity Insight described in this episode, that collects and organizes both structured and unstructured data about customers across multiple sources to build a comprehensive profile of the customer journey.
