Terms defined in this episode
Hyper-localized content
Content that references specific local landmarks, neighborhoods, or area-specific data. Google's October algorithm update explicitly rewarded this type of content for local service businesses.
LLM.txt
A file some sites add to signal instructions to large language models, discussed in the episode as a debated tactic whose actual impact on LLM citation visibility is still unclear.
Recharge days
A policy at Dmitrii's agency allowing team members to take an unplanned day off when they feel unable to do their best work, intended to improve output quality and mental well-being.
