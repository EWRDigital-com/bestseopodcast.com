8 Elite Digital Marketing Trends to watch out for in 2020 by Matthew Bertram #476

Ep. 47621 min2020-03-17

In 2020, digital marketers should focus on four action steps: platform storytelling, building real relationships over chasing likes, deploying chat bots, and working with micro influencers to generate measurable revenue.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Stories on every platform add urgency and engagement, especially since Snapchat and Instagram stories disappear after posting.
  • Stop chasing likes and focus on real relationships; personal posts tend to generate the most audience engagement.
  • Deploy a chat bot or live chat immediately, and test it on friends and family before going live.
  • Micro influencers can each generate around a thousand dollars a month in revenue, so scale by recruiting more of them.
  • For products with low search volume, influencer marketing is one of the best channels to get the message out.

Questions this episode answers

Why should marketers use stories on social platforms in 2020?

Stories on platforms like Snapchat and Instagram disappear after posting, which creates a sense of urgency that drives people to view and engage immediately. Different audiences on different platforms may need different versions of the story, and some versions may be video while others are content focused.

Should businesses hide a chat bot when no one is available to respond?

According to the episode, keeping the bot visible even when no one is live and showing a form asking users to leave a message actually generates a meaningful number of submissions, making it function as a more convenient contact page. A live response is ideal but not strictly required.

How do micro influencers work as a marketing strategy?

Micro influencers may not have massive audiences but can each generate around a thousand dollars a month in revenue for a product. The strategy is to recruit enough of them, for example 15 influencers at a thousand dollars each equals fifteen thousand dollars a month, and build sponsorship arrangements with those whose audiences respond best.

Why is influencer marketing especially important for products with low search volume?

When very few people are actively searching for a product or its core benefit, search-based SEO generates little traffic. Influencer marketing becomes one of the primary ways to get the message in front of potential customers who are not yet searching for the product.

Terms defined in this episode
Micro influencer
A social media creator without massive mainstream appeal who can still drive targeted sales, used in the episode to describe individuals generating around a thousand dollars a month in product revenue.
Chat bot
An automated messaging tool placed on a website or Facebook page that answers visitor questions, routes them through a decision tree, and can collect contact information when no live agent is available.
Stories
Short-form content posted on platforms like Snapchat and Instagram that disappears after a set time, creating urgency and encouraging immediate viewer engagement.
