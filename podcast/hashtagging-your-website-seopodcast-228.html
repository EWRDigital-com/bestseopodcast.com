Questions this episode answers

How do you optimize a hashtag for Google search?

Create a page dedicated to that hashtag, place the hashtag in the page title and meta description, and promote it consistently across your site and social channels. This increases the likelihood the page shows up when people search that hashtag on Google.

How can you increase Google traffic without building links?

The episode covers 12 tactics from a Moz article by Cyrus Shepard, including in-depth articles, rich snippets, video optimization, Google authorship, site speed improvements, smartphone SEO, international audience expansion, social annotations on Google Plus, snippet optimization, and ongoing on-page SEO.

Why does site speed matter for SEO and conversions?

Site speed is described as one of Google's top algorithm ranking components. A faster site raises your ranking likelihood. One client example in the episode showed conversions rise by around 30 percent after code cleanup and condensed style sheets made the site load a couple seconds faster.

What is schema markup used for beyond search results?

Schema markup lets you control the image and content pulled when your URL is shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, and other social sites. You can define a specific branded image so you are not at the mercy of whatever image those platforms auto-select from your page.

Terms defined in this episode
Rich snippets
Structured data markup added to pages so search engines can display enhanced results; required for events to appear in the Google carousel, and also controls images shown when a URL is shared on social platforms.
Google authorship
A setup linking content to a Google Plus author profile; the episode notes authorship visibility improves when the author is actively posting, sharing, and getting plus ones on their personal Google Plus page.
Snippet optimization
Crafting titles under 70 characters and meta descriptions that include a call to action or phone number so users can convert directly from the search engine results page.
In-depth articles
Long, valuable content pieces, such as how-to guides, that use authorship and pagination to generate multiple pages of quality content and improve Google traffic without link building.
