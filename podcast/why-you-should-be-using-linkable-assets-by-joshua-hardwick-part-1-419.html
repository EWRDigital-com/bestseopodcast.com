Questions this episode answers

What is a linkable asset in SEO?

A linkable asset is a piece of content purposely crafted to attract backlinks from other websites, usually in your niche. Examples include infographics and online calculators. They are almost never product pages, and they work by giving other sites a reason to link back to you.

How do you build links with infographics?

Search Google for your topic plus the word infographic, filtered to results published in the last 12 months, to find sites that actively publish infographics. Then pitch your infographic to those sites. You can also submit to infographic directories, though those links tend to be nofollow.

How do you promote an online tool or calculator to earn backlinks?

Find pages that already discuss the problem your tool solves and pitch them your calculator as a better experience for their visitors. You can also run low-cost PPC ads such as Facebook ads to build awareness, which can indirectly result in links from people who discover the tool.

Why should you create linkable assets instead of just promoting regular content?

According to the article covered in this episode, linkable assets have a much higher ROI on promotion efforts because they are created with attracting backlinks in mind, not just conversions. Backlinks act as votes that improve organic rankings and drive referral traffic.

Terms defined in this episode
Linkable asset
A piece of content purposely crafted with the intention of attracting links, usually from other websites in your niche, almost never a product page.
Link juice
The ranking value passed from one page to another through a hyperlink, which can boost the authority of a linked product page when the linking page has many backlinks.
Referring domains
Unique domains that link back to a page, distinct from total backlinks, as multiple links can come from the same domain.
Boolean search
A Google search technique using special symbols or operators to narrow results, such as searching for a topic plus the word infographic filtered by title and date.
