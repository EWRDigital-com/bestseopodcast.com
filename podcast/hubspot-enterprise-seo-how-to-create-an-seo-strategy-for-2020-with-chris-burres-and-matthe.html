Ep. 49330 min2020-08-10

A good SEO strategy for 2020 centers on pillar content, topic clusters, consistent blogging, link building, image compression, staying current on SEO news, and tracking organic traffic and rankings with tools like SEMrush.

Apple PodcastsSpotifyYouTubeiHeartRadioRSS
Key takeaways
  • Build pillar content from a topic list, then create long-tail keyword clusters and dedicated pages for each topic.
  • Link building is the primary off-page SEO activity, using content marketing, email outreach, broken backlink building, and public relations.
  • Compress all images before uploading, using tools like TinyPNG or Google Squoosh, to keep files in kilobytes and protect page speed.
  • Historically optimize old blog posts monthly, updating information, alt tags, and meta descriptions while watching for changing keyword intent.

About this episode

Questions this episode answers

What are the key steps in an SEO strategy for 2020 according to HubSpot?

The steps include making a list of topics for pillar content, creating long-tail keyword lists for topic clusters, building pages per topic, setting up a blog, blogging consistently, creating a link building plan, compressing media, staying current on SEO news, and measuring content success with tools like SEMrush.

How should you track the success of your SEO content?

Track overall organic traffic, how pages rank for each long-tail keyword, index pages, leads, ROI, inbound links, and actual SERP rankings. SEMrush is recommended as a tool. Reviewing this data can reveal unexpected trends that lead to strong marketing strategies.

How important is updating old content as part of an ongoing SEO strategy?

Devoting time each month to revisit old blog posts, add updated information, fix alt tags, improve meta descriptions, and add editorial value is a core part of the ongoing SEO process. Tracking changing keywords and new search intent during these reviews is also recommended.

In their words

Link building is the process of building one-way hyperlinks or backlinks from a website, the goal of improving search engine visibility. Common link building strategies include content marketing, building useful tools, email outreach, broken backlink building, and public relations.

Terms defined in this episode
Pillar Content
Content built around a broad topic list that serves as the foundation for an SEO strategy, with subtopics organized into topic clusters.
Topic Clusters
Groups of related long-tail keyword pages that link back to a pillar content page, helping establish topical authority.
Link Building
The process of attracting inbound links, also called backlinks, to your website from elsewhere on the web, and the primary objective of off-page SEO.
Historically Optimizing Content
A monthly practice of revisiting old blog posts to add updated information, fix alt tags, improve meta descriptions, and reflect changing keyword intent.
