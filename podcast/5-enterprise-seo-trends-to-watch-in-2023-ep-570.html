About this episode

SEO is changing. From search updates and algorithm changes to advancements in AI and conversational search, check out these trends to stay ahead.

Questions this episode answers

What is compounding SEO value and why does it matter for enterprises?

Compounding SEO value means the work done today continues paying off in future years. Data from Bright Edge tracking the top 1000 e-commerce terms showed that top-ranked pages drove 20% more keywords and 120% more traffic year over year, with user engagement building authority over time.

How does Google handle AI-generated content in 2023?

Google stated it will still rank AI-generated content if it is helpful and users are looking for it, with no penalty attached yet. However, Google can easily identify AI content, and since AI can only write what has already been written, content lacking originality risks being un-indexed or ignored.

What is E-E-A-T and why is it important for enterprise SEO?

E-E-A-T stands for Experience, Expertise, Authority, and Trustworthiness. Google uses this framework to determine which sources carry more weight on a topic. Proving experience through schema, authorship, video testimonials, and matching online profiles to real-world credentials is increasingly important in search rankings.

What role do data lakes play in enterprise SEO strategy?

Data lakes are large repositories of raw data that can be processed and analyzed to extract valuable insights. According to Salesforce, nearly four in five marketers believe data quality and intelligence are among the most important factors driving marketing performance, making them vital for enterprise SEO decision-making.
