Questions this episode answers

How important are backlinks for SEO in 2024?

According to Matt Bertram, everything Google does is built on backlinks and they are not going away. They act as signals that someone is voting for your site, but Google evaluates whether each link is legitimate, purchased, or passes any ranking value at all.

What is topical authority and why does it matter for rankings?

Topical authority means establishing expertise across a subject area so Google categorizes your site for related terms. Matt Bertram explains that sites already ranking for topical authority can appear in results quickly, while sites not yet in the index may take 60 days or more to be categorized.

Should you delete low quality content from your website?

Matt Bertram advises against simply deleting low quality content because doing so can change the link equity of your site. Instead, improve it to high quality, combine it with related content, redirect it to a more valuable page, or drive it back to the parent page.

How do core web vitals affect Google rankings?

Matt Bertram estimates page experience and core web vitals account for roughly 30 to 40 percent of total ranking. He recommends treating Search Console errors as a roadmap and working to get that error count to zero as a foundational step.

Terms defined in this episode
E-E-A-T
Expertise, Experience, Authoritativeness, and Trust signals that Google uses to evaluate content quality, with experience added to address AI-generated content that lacks first-hand proof.
Core Web Vitals
Google ranking factors covering site speed, interactivity, and visual stability, which Matt Bertram estimates make up roughly 30 to 40 percent of total ranking weight.
Topical Authority
A site's demonstrated depth of expertise on a subject area, which helps Google categorize and rank that site faster for related keywords.
Helpful Content Update
A Google algorithm update targeting content that lacks genuine usefulness or first-hand experience, rewarding content that proves the author actually engaged with the subject.
