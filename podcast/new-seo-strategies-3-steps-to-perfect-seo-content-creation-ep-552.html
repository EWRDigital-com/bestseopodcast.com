SEO content creation now requires three steps: empathetic people-first content, topic clustering to answer all related questions, and subject matter experts to build authority and attract backlinks.

Key takeaways
  • Write people-first content by focusing on user intent, personalizing for your audience, and telling stories that lower sales resistance.
  • Topic clusters answer all questions around a topic, help with internal linking, attract backlinks, and future-proof your SEO strategy.
  • Subject matter experts improve E-A-T scores, build trust, make content unique, and create organic backlink opportunities.
By writing people first content, you stay aligned with Google's narrative. That great content is the best way to protect yourself from updates.

About this episode

Learn how to set up an effective content strategy to deliver a high return on your marketing investment efforts in 2022.

Questions this episode answers

What are the three steps to perfect SEO content creation?

The three steps are: first, empathetic people-first content that focuses on user intent and storytelling; second, topic clustering to answer all questions around a topic; and third, incorporating subject matter experts to boost expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness and attract backlinks.

What is a topic cluster and why does it matter for SEO?

A topic cluster is a group of content resources that answer all the questions around a particular topic. According to the episode, topic clusters help future-proof SEO, scale strategy, support internal linking, attract backlinks, increase web engagement, and increase conversions.

Should AI be used to write SEO content?

The episode advises proceeding cautiously. AI is best used as a supporting tool to help writers overcome writer's block, create outlines, and generate summaries. A human should remain involved, and AI content should support rather than replace primary human-written content.

Terms defined in this episode
Topic Clusters

E-A-T
Stands for expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness. A scoring factor that subject matter experts help improve, especially important for your money and your life content.
