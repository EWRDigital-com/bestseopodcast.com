What is CIVO?

Answered across 7 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

Search Engine Visitor Optimization, eWebStyle's term for optimizing calls to action on a site, such as capturing email addresses or phone numbers from visitors.

How experts on the show define it

Search Engine Visitor Optimization, eWebStyle's term for optimizing calls to action on a site, such as capturing email addresses or phone numbers from visitors.

From We discuss the top 10 SEO mistakes - Unknown Secrets of SEO E-Webstyle Number 65

The hosts' term for optimizing the meta description as a first opportunity to convince a searcher to click through to your site after seeing it in search results.

From Optimizing Page Titles for Algorithm Changes - #seopodast 171

A phrase coined by eWebStyle meaning what you do with a visitor after they arrive from a search engine, focusing on user experience beyond just ranking.

From How not to be a douche when creating inbound links - Unknown Secrets of SEO E-Webstyle Number 60

A term coined by eWebStyle meaning the practice of designing and optimizing a website for the human visitor first, with the principle that CIVO begins at the search engine results page.

From Facebook, User Generated Content, Keyword Selector Tool - Unknown Secrets of SEO E-Webstyle Number 62

A concept discussed in the podcast referring to optimizing what happens after visitors arrive, including calls to action, education, and newsletter sign-ups.

From Double Opt-in E-mail List for SEO - Unknown Secrets of SEO E-Webstyle Number 51

A term used in this episode for optimizing the meta description to compel searchers who see it on the results page to click through to the site.

From How should I use SEO? - #seopodcast 160

An eWebStyle term for optimizing what happens to a visitor after they arrive on your website from a search engine, equivalent to the industry term conversion optimization.
