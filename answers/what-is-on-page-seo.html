What is On-page SEO?

Answered across 4 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

Sometimes called on-site SEO, it is the process of tweaking a page's content, tags, and internal links to improve search visibility and increase traffic.

How experts on the show define it

Sometimes called on-site SEO, it is the process of tweaking a page's content, tags, and internal links to improve search visibility and increase traffic.

From 12 Essential On-Page SEO Factors You Need To Know Ep. 541

Optimization focused on three core elements: content, HTML code, and site architecture, which together help pages rank and load correctly.

From Social Media Facts - What You Can Do - #seopodcast 217

The practice of optimizing individual page elements including URLs, titles, image tags, and written content to target the right keywords and improve search rankings.

From E-Commerce SEO Tips - Internet Marketing Podcast Number 198

Optimization work done directly on a website including titles, meta information, link sculpting, navigation, page count, and content generation.
