What is NAP?

Answered across 19 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

Name, address, and phone number. These must be consistently and accurately listed across hundreds of local search engines and directories to support local SEO rankings.

How experts on the show define it

Name, address, and phone number. These must be consistently and accurately listed across hundreds of local search engines and directories to support local SEO rankings.

From 12 Proven Local SEO Tips To Dominate The SERPs And Map Pack Ep. 567

Name, address, and phone number. Must be identical across all local listings and citations to ensure continuity for local SEO.

From 12 Essential On-Page SEO Factors You Need To Know Part 2 Ep. 542

Name, address, and phone number, the core citation elements Google uses to verify a business identity and distinguish it from other businesses with similar names.

From Ranking Locally: Your Guide to GMB & SEO (Unknown Secrets Revealed!) Ep. 580

Name, address, and phone number. These three data points must be exactly the same across every online listing, website page, and Google My Business profile for a business location.

From Episode 515: Local SEO Strategy for Multiple Locations: Everything You Need To Know by Aaron Agius #515

Name, address, and phone number of a business, which must be consistent across all platforms and ideally marked up with schema so it can be indexed and displayed correctly.

From The Perfect Local SEO Landing Page by Marcus Miller #329

Name, Address, and Phone number. The episode recommends keeping NAP consistent across your site, Google Plus page, Yelp, and any other directory to help rank in Google Maps local listings.

From #SEOpodcast 274 - Investing in your Content

Name, address, and phone number. For local SEO, this should appear in video annotations, the video description, and be spoken aloud in the video audio so Google can transcribe and index it.

From Youtube and SEO for Local - #SEOpodcast 235

Name, address, and phone number, the core contact details that must appear consistently on every page of a local business website and across all online listings.

Name, address, and phone number, the core business information that should be consistent across all local listings and marked up with schema on a website.

Name, Address, and Phone number. Must be exactly consistent, down to parentheses and abbreviations, across every directory listing, social profile, and domain registration to help a brand own page one.

Name, address, and phone number. Must be identical across all online directories and listings for local SEO consistency.
