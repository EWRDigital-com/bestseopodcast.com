What is FAB?

Answered across 6 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

A sales framework standing for Features, Advantages, and Benefits, used in the episode to suggest going beyond just benefits when writing meta descriptions.

How experts on the show define it

A sales framework standing for Features, Advantages, and Benefits, used in the episode to suggest going beyond just benefits when writing meta descriptions.

From #SEOpodcast 276 - Meta Description Tips

Feature, advantage, and benefits. A persuasion framework used in content to show readers what a product does, why it matters, and how it benefits them.

From 10 Things Your Content Must Do to Succeed by Lucinda Honeycutt #380

A sales framework covering features, advantages, and benefits. In PPC ad copy with limited characters, the episode recommends focusing only on the benefit.

From Improving Ad Copy Conversion - #seopodcast 226

Features, Advantages, and Benefits. A content framework for addressing a user's pain point by explaining what a product or service does, why it is better, and how it directly benefits the reader.

From Why No One is Linking Back to You - #SEOpodcast 259

Feature, advantage, benefit. An old school sales framework the hosts recommend applying to landing page copy to speak to what users gain from a product or service.

From Tips to Avoiding High Bounce Rates - #SEOpodcast 252

Feature, Advantage, Benefit. A sales framework the hosts recommend including in bullet points on product and service pages to drive conversions.
