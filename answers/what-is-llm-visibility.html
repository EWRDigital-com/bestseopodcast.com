What is LLM visibility?

Answered across 11 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

The goal of having a business or brand appear in responses generated by large language models like ChatGPT, treated by EWR as a ranking target separate from traditional search engines.

How experts on the show define it

The goal of having a business or brand appear in responses generated by large language models like ChatGPT, treated by EWR as a ranking target separate from traditional search engines.

From The New Era of Digital Strategy & AI-Powered Growth with Matthew Bertram

A catch-all term used in the episode referring to marketing and analytics work aimed at ensuring a brand is found, cited, and accurately represented by large language model search platforms.

From Why AI Overviews Changed Your Traffic And What To Do Next With Connor Kimball

The degree to which a brand or piece of content is surfaced or referenced by large language model systems such as ChatGPT or Perplexity, distinct from traditional search engine rankings.

From Why Old SEO Metrics Fail And What To Track Instead With Corey Morris

A term used in this episode to describe how well a brand is recognized, cited, and surfaced by large language models across top, middle, and bottom of funnel prompts.

From What Actually Drives Citations in LLMs With Solomon Thimothy

A framework described by the host for ensuring a business or brand is discoverable and cited by large language models like ChatGPT and Perplexity, treated as a distinct discipline from traditional SEO.

From Pairing SEO With Automation With Steven Werley

The ability of a business or brand to appear and be cited within large language model generated answers, discussed in this episode as an emerging but not yet mature channel for legacy service businesses.

From AI-First Marketing For A Legacy Industry With Renaud Delaquis

A framework for optimizing content so that large language models surface and cite a brand or solution as the optimal answer, distinct from traditional Google SEO ranking.

From How To Use AI Without Getting Deindexed With Jon Gillham

How a brand or business appears and is described within large language model AI tools like ChatGPT, which users now consult to verify or research brands they discover elsewhere.

From Why Traffic Is Down but Leads Are Up, and How to Read the Signals With Charlie Grinnell

Being discoverable and cited within large language model platforms like ChatGPT, treated in this episode as the near-term evolution beyond traditional Google SEO.

From From Podcasting To AI: Building Real Audiences That Convert With Chris Krimitsos

The ability of a brand to be surfaced and cited by large language models such as ChatGPT or Copilot, shaped by the quality and authority of third-party sources that mention the brand online.

From Guardrails For Your Online Brand With Paige Donnell

The ability of a brand or agency to appear as a cited or recommended option inside large language models like ChatGPT when users search or research topics related to that brand.
