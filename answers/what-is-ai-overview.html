What is AI Overview?

Answered across 5 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

A Google feature that displays AI-generated answers at the top of search results. Google implemented it in response to losing search traffic to AI tools like ChatGPT.

How experts on the show define it

A Google feature that displays AI-generated answers at the top of search results. Google implemented it in response to losing search traffic to AI tools like ChatGPT.

From Thriving Through Unforeseen Challenges with Internet Marketing Trends in 2025 (Pod You Can Skip Without Guilt)

Google's feature that displays AI-generated answer summaries at the top of search results, launched broadly around March or April 2025, which reduced click-through traffic to informational blog content.

From Why AI Overviews Changed Your Traffic And What To Do Next With Connor Kimball

A Google search feature that displays an AI-generated summary at the top of results; Lindsay Halsey notes agencies can track how often a site appears in these using tools like Wincher.

From Visibility Beats Visits: Rethinking Marketing For The LLM Era With Lindsay Halsey

A Google search feature that lets AI answer a search query directly on the results page, reducing the need for users to click through to any website.

From AI Revolution in Marketing with Kevin Surace

A Google search feature that is described in the episode as tanking website traffic by answering queries directly, reducing clicks to individual sites.

From Guardrails For Your Online Brand With Paige Donnell
