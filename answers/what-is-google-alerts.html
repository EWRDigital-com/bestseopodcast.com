What is Google Alerts?

Answered across 9 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

A Google tool that notifies you via a link anytime Google indexes new content containing a phrase you specify, useful for tracking your brand, competitors, or any keyword.

How experts on the show define it
