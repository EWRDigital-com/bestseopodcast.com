Why should I run SEO and PPC at the same time instead of choosing one?

Answered across 3 episodes of The Best SEO Podcast

Edited and curated by Matthew Bertram, host of The Best SEO Podcast (produced by EWR Digital).

The short answer

Running both together means different audiences see you in paid and organic results with very little overlap, so total traffic increases. PPC also sends user-behavior signals to search engines that can accelerate organic ranking movement, and both channels share keyword and content insights that reduce wasted spend.

From Minor Panda Refresh - #seopodcast 135

Yes. The hosts recommend doing both PPC and organic SEO together. They cite a test where someone turned off PPC and tracked the impact with statistics before turning it back on, suggesting that running both simultaneously produces better overall results than relying on one alone.
